Who We Are

We are not just another startup

Dynamixity is a professional development platform for youths and undergraduates, enabling them take bold steps to achieving their dreams. At Dynamixity students can learn professional skills from one another, be inspired to create solutions for our local problems with global skills, which will put them in the best position for career opportunities beyond school.

Our mission & vision as an organisation

Our Mission

To be the largest community of focused and dynamic students in Africa, to help attain personal and professional development aimed towards gainful employment and career.

Our Vision

To produce creative and intelligent students with adeptness in technologies, that suit the fastest paced work environment.Students who will create the next big opportunities in Africa..
